A first go at implementing the Dai et al model From the supplement to 10.1126/science.1219805, we have: [n_{t+1} = n_t g(n_t + \epsilon n_t, \theta)] [g(nt) = \frac{n{t+1}}{n_t}] This model is based on two phases of daily growth: a slow exponential growth phase at low cell densities, followed by a logistic growth phase with a higher per capita growth rate at intermediate cell densities. This model has 5 parameters: T lag is the lag time before yeast cells start to grow after being transferred into new media (the total time for daily growth is 23 hours).
