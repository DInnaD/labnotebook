UPS and data vs optimal control

Random idea for possible further exploration: The use of ‘big data’ by UPS to perform lots of small efficiency gains seems to be everybody’s favorite example (NPR, The Economist). During a typical applications of optimal control for ecological conservation talk yesterday I couldn’t help thinking back to that story. The paradigm shift is not so much the kind or amount of the data being used as it is the control levers themselves.
