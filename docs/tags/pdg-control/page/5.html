In advance of our upcoming conference call for the Pretty Darn Good Control (pdg-control) NIMBioS working group, I had a few remaining issues to touch up in Figures 3 and 4. Figure 3 shows harvest and stock dynamics under the different functional forms fo the cost. The goal is to provide a very intuitive, visual sense of how the policies differ under these different functional forms, such as a fixed “transaction fee” (fixed) or a cost that is proportional to the size of the change in policy (L1), or the square of that change (L2), etc.
