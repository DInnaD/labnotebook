Sun, Jun 23, 2013

This post creates new tag eml for posts discussing the Ecological Markup Langauge or the implementation of an R parser thereof (reml). Why EML? Jones et al.(2005) give an excellent explanation describing the relative advantage of the descriptive metadata approach over vertically integrated repositories (such as Genbank, TreeBase, or many other repostories that have been the focus of rOpenSci development to date). In short, the EML approach allows archiving of very heterogeneous data without having to standardize everything into a narrow and pre-defined syntax.
